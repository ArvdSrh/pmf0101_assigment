Who are we?

Launched in 2021, Our technology platform connects customers and restaurant partners, serving their multiple needs. Customers use our platform to search and discover restaurants, malaysian cuisines, local restaurants, and read and write customer generated reviews. Our honest reviews makes it easier for new customers to know more about the restaurants and dishes that they're looking for. Besides that, we also allow customers to give their own review which will be much efficient since there's many restaurants that we also never heard of.
